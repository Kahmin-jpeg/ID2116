Week 3

This time we managed to uncover more about Javascript using p5.js and its application on Mircrobits.


We started out with in-class assignments on how p5.js functions. We went ahead to do some brainstorming and bouncing ideas to how we wish to design a product with an interactive feedback.

After going through a list of input and output, we settled on accelerating to play music and lights metaphoring the old jazz period and in turn enhancing the existing cleaning experiences.

We learned that the LED had colours and functionality that does not corresponds to the ideal colours that caused a bit of confusion at first
E.g Neutral (Black), Ground (Green) and Live (Red)

After solving the initial challenges we faced with the LED aspect as the output, we quickly moved onto exploring the possible music output when the broom is moved.

This shows the exploration we had done after considering the possible input / output learned during and after lesson! Our next steps would likely be exploring an improved output feedback.
